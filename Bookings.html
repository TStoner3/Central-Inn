Welcome to Central-Inn!

At Central-Inn, we offer a variety of room types to suit your needs. Our rooms can house up to four people and are designed with your comfort in mind featuring comfortable bedding, Wi-Fi, and other modern amenities. To get started, please select your desired dates of stay and the number of guests. Our team will match you with the perfect room available, along with the rate.

Single Room

A cozy room perfect for a solo traveler. The room features a comfortable twin-sized bed, a desk, and a private bathroom. Amenities include complimentary Wi-Fi, cable TV, and air conditioning.

Double Room

A spacious room perfect for couples or friends traveling together. The room features two comfortable full-sized beds, a desk, and a private bathroom. Amenities include complimentary Wi-Fi, cable TV, and air conditioning.

Triple Room

A great option for families or small groups. The room features three comfortable twin-sized beds, a desk, and a private bathroom. Amenities include complimentary Wi-Fi, cable TV, and air conditioning.

Family Room

A spacious room perfect for families or groups of up to four. The room features a comfortable king-sized bed and two twin-sized beds, a desk, and a private bathroom. Amenities include complimentary Wi-Fi, cable TV, and air conditioning.
