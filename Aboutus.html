Our History

Central Inn is a hotel with a great history and boasts one of the most historical locations in New Britain.
Started as a family business, Central Inn has become much more from the humble beginnings in which it started.
Rich in history Central Inn was one of the first hotels constructed in New Britain and has been the most successful since.
With expansions annually Central Inn has taken its hotel model to a new level while keeping the homey feel and charm it once had.
